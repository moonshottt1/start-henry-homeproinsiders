Why comparing quotes matters

The same roofing project can come back with quotes that vary by thousands of dollars. That's normal — different roofers have different overhead, crews, and material suppliers. The point of comparing isn't just to find the cheapest bid; it's to:

  • See where prices actually land in your area, not what a home improvement video told you they should be.
  • Spot suspiciously low quotes — usually a sign the roofer is underestimating or planning to make up the gap in change orders after the work starts.
  • Get a clear scope of work so you can compare apples-to-apples instead of guessing what's included.

Homeowners who get three or more written quotes typically end up paying less than those who accept the first bid. On a project this size, that comparison can mean the difference between staying on budget and going thousands over it.
